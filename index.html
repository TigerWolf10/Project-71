The Grammar School at Leeds

Places On Campus

Swimming Pool

The 25 meter-long pool is used by both junior and senior students. With diving blocks, changing rooms and a spectator area, this state-of-the-art pool is a fantastic place.


Library

The School Library is a giant, 2 floor room full of all sorts of books. There are also tablesfor studying and a room in which you can chill with your friends or get advice from the sixth-formers.


3G pitches

The 3G pitches are use for both boys' and girls's rugby as well as the numerous rugby matches held throughout the season.

  Inter-school Debate
House Debating

In this inter-school event, students from diferrent houses get the oppurtunity to debate against other houses in teams of 3. You do not have to have debating experience to participate, and is great fun to do!


  House Music Competition
House Music Competition

Instrumentalists of all levels can perform during this event, competeing with people of a similar level. Even just Entering the competition earns points for your house.

Wind in The Willows

With a cast of students ranging from year 7-9, this stunning play is just one of many stuents have to oppurtunity to partake in.
